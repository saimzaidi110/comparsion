Set Clear Goals

Whether you're trying to build muscle, lose fat, or improve endurance, having specific goals will help guide your workout plan and track progress.

Warm Up Properly

Never skip your warm-up! Doing 5-10 minutes of light cardio (like jogging or cycling) followed by dynamic stretches will prepare your muscles and joints for the workout.

Progressive Overload

To see improvements, you need to gradually increase the intensity of your workouts. This could mean adding more weight, increasing reps, or doing more sets over time
